This page describes the terms of participation for the MIT Social Media Study. The study was approved by the MIT IRB under protocol number 2506001673.

<strong>Terms of Participation</strong>

Participation in this study is voluntary. You may choose to not participate, skip questions, or stop participating at any time.

To participate, you must be at least 18 years old.

The survey takes about 5 minutes. Eligible invited participants are offered compensation in Amazon gift cards for completing the survey. Compensation is limited to one eligible completion per person.

TikTok, X/Twitter, or YouTube Login is used only for eligibility verification and to record basic details about your account (such as your number of followers or subscribers and number of posts or videos) to link to the survey responses; it does not give the study access to post on your account, read private messages, or see your password.

<strong>Who Is Conducting This Study</strong>

This is an academic research study conducted by Shakked Noy, an Economics PhD student at MIT. The study asks social media creators about their experience producing content, including how much time they spend creating content, whether and how they earn money from content production, and what motivates them to post.

<strong>Voluntary Participation</strong>

Taking part is voluntary. You do not have to take the survey, and you can stop at any point. Where the survey allows it, you can also skip questions.

If you do not want us to contact you again about this study, reply to the invitation and say so. We will record that request and remove you from future outreach for this study.
