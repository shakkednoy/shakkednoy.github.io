The MIT Social Media Study is an academic research study about how social media creators produce content, how much time they spend creating content, whether and how they earn money from content production, and what motivates them to post.

The study was approved by the MIT IRB under protocol number 2506001673. Eligible invited participants are asked to complete a short survey and may be asked to verify basic account information from TikTok, X/Twitter, or YouTube.

Platform login is used only to verify eligibility and record basic account information for the study. The study does not ask for platform passwords, does not post to participants' accounts, and does not access private messages.
